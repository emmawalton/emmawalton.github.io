Learn more About Me

Emma Walton grew up in Atlanta, Georgia, where she learned family and community values. At the age of 12, she moved to San Diego, California with her four older siblings, parents, and two dogs. Emma learned to love the beach, sunshine, and being outside. After graduating from Poway High School in 2019, Emma began attending Brigham Young University. A year later, she served an 18-month mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Santiago, Chile. Due to COVID-19 restrictions, Emma focused a large portion of missionary efforts to online teaching through Facebook ads and Zoom lessons.

All of these experiences have brought Emma a great love for working with diverse people and helping to bring value to something she is passionate about. These interests have helped Emma decide to major in Marketing in order to work with the people, brands, and products.
